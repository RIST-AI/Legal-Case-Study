Welcome to the Ristaway Ag Co Case Study

In this simulation, you will take on the role of a compliance consultant for Ristaway Ag Co, a growing agricultural business. Your task is to review their financial reports, legal documents, and insurance policies to ensure they are meeting all legal requirements and managing risks properly.

How to Use This Simulation

This simulation covers a six-month period from January to June 2024. Each month, you'll face new situations and have to make decisions.

You'll need to:

  • Read the information carefully
  • Look at documents when needed
  • Answer questions by selecting the best option
  • Learn from the feedback after each decision

First, let's review the farm's basic documents to understand their situation.

Key Terms

Here are some important words you'll see in this simulation:

  • Compliance: Following all the rules and laws that apply to a business
  • Partnership: A business owned by two or more people who share profits and responsibilities
  • Liability: Legal responsibility for something, especially costs or damages
  • Superannuation: Money paid by employers into a fund for workers' retirement (also called "super")
